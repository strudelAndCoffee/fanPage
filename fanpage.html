RUSH, The Greatest Band Ever!


Band Website

The Canadian band RUSH exhibits the utmost of musicality, progressive song crafting, and outstanding attitudes as human beings.

Geddy Lee fronts the group as singer, bassist, and keyboardist. Alex Lifeson plays guitar, and Neil Peart (pronounced 'peert') plays drums, percussion, and is the main lyricist of the band. In January of 2020, Peart died of glioblastoma at the age of 67. R.I.P.

Their two bets known albums are 2112 (1976) and Moving Pictures (1981), both of which display their mastery of progressive rock exploration fused with radio-friendly pop rock sensibility.
